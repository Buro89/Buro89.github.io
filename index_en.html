WELKOM

Spreekuur 89 is a collective committed to protecting and creating the freedom of groups and individuals who are oppressed because they are considered 'suspicious' in the society. Not as official suspects in a criminal law sence, but as a 'risk population' because they are not conforming to the 'mainstream'.

Services for psychiatrized people

Spreekuur 89 is specialized in the case of psychiatrized people. We think that coercive psychiatric interventions are unacceptable and want to help building an alternative society that also provides freedom of psychiatrized people. We do this by providing support to individuals, by providing a strong and growing network of phone buddies, safe spaces and time-out places that will crowd out coercive services. We also provide emotional support and advice for people who are already in a coercive programme. Our services are totally independent from any formal organization, horizontally, informally and based on egalitarian principles. Besides this, we advice people on how to avoid coercion, without having to tell the person to obey to the norms of 'normality', but also by stimulating the wider society to selfreflect on their fear for the 'Other' and its obsession with extreme order and control. In all, we are against all oppression and scapegoating, and we are therefore open for questions from other oppressed groups as well.
