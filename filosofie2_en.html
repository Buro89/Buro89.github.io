Philosophy evenings

In 2018 we have started the discussion series 'Philosophy and Psychiatry'. The sessions are mostly at the last wednesday of the month. For May, we unfortunately had to forgo this because of a lot of events and duties of us all.
The evenings can be attended independently from attendance of each other. Stay up to date on this? The easiest way is by subscribing on the newsletter through an email to info@spreekuur89.nl. Preferrably, announce your presence in advance so we can see how many people there will be.

For whom?
For psychiatrized selves, for students or workers in care disciplines/ human sciences/ psychiatry, or people with multiple of these backgrounds, or people interested in critical philosophy on psychiatry, care and madness.

The facilitator of the evening will beJoeri Kooimans. He teaches philosophy at the Hogeschool Rotterdam to social work students and to internship students and workers of the Wegloophuis Utrecht. See his website here
